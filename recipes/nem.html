Nem Rán

These are the recipes to make nem rán (friend spring rolls)

Ingredients

  • Grounded Beef, Shrimp
  • Green Onions
  • Dried Wood Ear
  • Vermicelli
  • Carrots
  • Rice Paper
  • Eggs

Instruction

  1. Chop the Wood Ears, Carrots, and Vermicelli into small pieces
  2. Put the grounded beef, shrimp, green onions, carrots, wood ears, and vermicilli into a pot
  3. Crack an eggs and put it into the pot
  4. Mix all of them with a pair of chopsticks
  5. Place the mixture in sizable amounts on the rice papers
  6. Roll the rice paper, make sure it does not let the stuffing fall
  7. Deep-fry the rolls
  8. Enjoy
