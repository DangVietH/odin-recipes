Bánh Mì Ốp La (Omelette sandwich)

These are the recipes for Banh Mi Omelette

Ingredients

  • 1 crusty Baguette
  • 2 eggs
  • Cucumbers
  • Vietnamese cilantro
  • Tomatoes
  • Mayonnaise
  • Chili Sauce

Instruction

  1. Chop the tomatoes and cucumber into slices
  2. Wash the cilantro
  3. Cook the eggs into omelettes
  4. Mix the Mayonnaise and Chilli sauce together
  5. Create a cut in the Baguette (don't split it into two pieces)
  6. Spread the sauce in the bread
  7. Stuff the eggs -> tomatoes -> cucumber -> cilantro into the bread
  8. Enjoy
