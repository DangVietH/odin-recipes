Phở Bò

These are the recipes to make phở bò (beef phở) Hanoi Style

Ingredients

  • Beef & beef ribs
  • Lime
  • Green Onions
  • Pho noodles

Instruction

  1. Make the broth by using the beef ribs
  2. Chop the beef into slices
  3. Chop the green onions
  4. Place the noodles, beef slices, and green onions into a bowl
  5. Pour the Broth into the bowl
  6. Chop the lime into 2 and squeeze the juice into the bowl
  7. Enjoy
